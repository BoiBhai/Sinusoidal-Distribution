\subsection{Choice of Optimizer}
Following optimization techniques were tried and their drawbacks realized:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \texttt{optim}'s \texttt{L-BFGS-B} method: sensitivity on initial choices of parameters.
    \item \texttt{optim}'s \texttt{Nelder-Mead} method: unavailability of bounding boxes and the presence of unbounded solutions for certain problems such as $\N (0,1)$ fitting.
\end{enumerate}
